Immunization(argoprofile)
Definition

Describes the event of a patient being administered a vaccination or a record of a vaccination as reported by a patient, a clinician or another party and may include vaccine reaction information and what vaccination protocol was followed.

Control0..*
InvariantsDefined on this element
imm-1: If immunization was not administred (wasNotGiven=true) then there SHALL be no reaction nor explanation.reason present (xpath: not(f:wasNotGiven/@value=true() and (count(f:reaction) > 0 or exists(f:explanation/f:reason))))
imm-2: If immunization was administered (wasNotGiven=false) then explanation.reasonNotGiven SHALL be absent. (xpath: not(f:wasNotGiven/@value=false() and exists(f:explanation/f:reasonNotGiven)))
